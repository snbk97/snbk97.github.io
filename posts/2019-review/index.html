2019 Review

2019-12-31 :: 2 min read
#2019  #year-review 

2019 - The year of Keanu Reves memes, Tesla's cyber truck, Greta Thunberg's concern for the environment, ISRO's Chandrayaan-2 success et cetera

Well, It was a pretty good year for me as well. It was one of the year which had overwhelmed me with what it had to offer in both ways - positive and negative.

I learnt some very ancient but also few interesting tech in college like 6Bone, MobileIP, Computer graphics, Turing machines (almost flunked), last but not least, exciting tech : Java servlet 2.0 (/s)

Also I completed my 4th and 5th semesters in college, which is pretty great news.

Some how managed to get a software engineering internship and hope to get the full-time position also. The preparation for which somewhat a mixture of hard work and LOT OF LUCK. To be honest, it was fucking annoying at best, to go through at least 3-4 interviews (till the last round), just to hear the same sh*t “Better Luck Next Time. Kek”

JK, it was more like - Good luck for your next interviews, f*ck off now m8.

New habit, that I'm looking into - listening to podcasts and talk shows. People really talk about really fascinating things about life, thoughts, food, religion and most importantly TECH.

To name a few -

  • AI Podcast - Lex Fridman
  • If then else - Mayuko
  • Habit Coach - Ashdin Doctor
  • The Vinamre Kasanaa show - Vinamre Kasana
  • PG Radio - Prakhar Gupta

Coming to the end of this post, I'm left with 6 more months of college, which will be spent mostly in the internship and hopefully other productive things. Let's see what 2020 brings us.

Happy New Year !

~fin
