dgplug Homework

2019-06-18 :: 1 min read
#dgplug  #hack 

I joined dgplug foss summer training, few days back as I was quite fascinated with the concept of FOSS and Open Source in general. It is always my dream, to have my patch accepted in some FOSS project and watch it get used in daily life by others. It has been an amazing experience overall. There is a lot to learn in this world. I believe I have started taking small steps towards learning new stuff in this process.

Anyways, being in this training, there is a lot of material to read and comprehend. As the training progresses over IRC, it becomes cumbersome to keep tracks of the homework and links that we get to read.

So here's a little bookmarklet I made to help to figure out the links and messages containing the word homework.

  • Drag and place it on your bookmark bar.
  • Open IRC chat logs and click the bookmarklet.
  • Your homework for the day, await at the end.
