dgplug Homework

2019-06-18
#dgplug  #hack 

I joined dgplug foss summer training, few days back as I was quite fascinated with the concept of FOSS and Open Source in general. It is always my dream, to have my patch accepted in some FOSS project and watch it get used in daily life by others. It has been an amazing experince overall. There is a lot to learn in this world. I believe I have started taking small steps towards learning new stuff in this process.

Anyways, being in this training, there is a lot of material to read and comprehend. As the training progresses over IRC, it becomes cumbersome to keep tracks of the homeworks and links that we get to read.

So here’s a little bookmarklet I made to help to figure out the links and messages containing the word homework.

It is still recommended to read the chat atleast once after IRC sessions; the bookmarklet might miss out on few of the homeworks, which are not links.
